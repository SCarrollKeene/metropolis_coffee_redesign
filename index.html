Coffee and Community Since 2002

Metropolis Coffee is dedicated to creating a coffee experience that is welcoming, approachable, and reliable. We continually strive to better serve the neighborhoods around us and grow with our expanding mile-high city.

We are proud to be partnered with Herkimer Coffee in Seattle, WA. With so many coffee companies and amazing coffees around, we try to bring something out of the ordinary to Denver and look to showcase Herkimer's long-standing reputation and unique offerings.

We are dedicated to promoting and enhancing the barista craft locally and strive to make a better cup of coffee each day so your coffee experience will be the best it can be.
